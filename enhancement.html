Button Hover Effect

To make the website more interactive, button is the essential component to be added in. For the button design, we have made three different designs. One is for the connecting with other pages and other two are designed for the form decoration. Although it is said that consistency using the same design is critical point to make a good website, but different for those designs is actually helping users to have different visual effects and also differntiate the different uses. For the connection, the button background will be dark green and outline will disappear once hovering it while for the form, the button background will be darker and the outline still remain once hovering it.
