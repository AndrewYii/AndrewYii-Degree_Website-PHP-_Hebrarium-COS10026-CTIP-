The website works devices of any dimension. The responsive layout of the website allow it to be viewed from any window size. Therefore, it can work with PC monitors, laptop screens, tablets and even mobile browsers.

The chatbot is not a GIF image. It is designed using html and css. Responsive Navbar & Hamburger Icon

The navbar designed not only connects the homepage with other pages, but it also comes with a search bar which can be connected with our Assignment2. Once the users hover to the navbar section, the particular section or text will be underlined and further showing more information in a new frame. The users can then hover the information displayed and click on them to explore those pages. Moreover, when the page is viewed on smaller screens, the navbar will transform into the hamburger icon. The hamburger icon makes all the orientation of the selection more organised and user-friendly.

Button Hover Effect

To make the website more interactive, button is one of the essential components to be added in. We have three different designs for all the buttons used in our entire website. The first type is to link a specific page to other pages and the remaining two are designed for the form decoration. Although it is said that maintaining consistency using the same design is critical to make a good website, but variations of designs will give users different visual effects. For the button integrated with hyperlink effect, the button will become dark green and its outline will disappear once the user hover over it; while as for the button in the form, the background will turn darker and the outline will still remain once it is being hovered.

Automatic Typing is added to make important messages visually stand out. Additionally, the effect lends personality by emulating a more engaged and human tone of speech. Content that is presented dynamically entices readers to interact with the material more.

Back To Top Button

By reducing the need for manual scrolling and enabling users to swiftly scroll back to the top with a single click, the Back to Top button improves user experience and navigation on lengthy web pages. It enhances accessibility, particularly for mobile users, and facilitates the effective exploration of extensive information by users, making the webpage more navigable and user-friendly.
